Research Question:

How does engagement with a secondary audience impact valuation by a candidate’s primary audience?

Primary Audience Considerations: Capability and Commitment

Capability

  • When uncertain about candidate quality, primary audiences may view secondary audience engagement as an index of ability

=> Positive evaluation

 

(Fini, Jourdan, Perkmann, 2018)

Primary Audience Considerations: Capability and Commitment

Commitment

  • Where audience interests  potentially conflict, primary audiences may view secondary audience engagement as betrayal of identity

=> Negative evaluation

 

(Phillips, Turco, and Zuckerman 2013; Fini, Jourdan, Perkmann, 2018)

Alternate Consideration: Brokerage Fitness

Representative Brokerage

(Gould & Fernandez, 1989)

  • Primary (peer) audience may view secondary audience engagement as a bid to broker
  • Peers evaluate the representativeness of the candidate
  • Aligned => Positive evaluation
  • Misaligned => Negative evaluation
